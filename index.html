Boxed Out, with its vibrant palettes and infinitely complex puzzles (that are easy to get, but difficult to master), is humankind’s greatest hope against boredom. Use your intuition to guide Cubey through futuristic architecture and hand-crafted structure, while constantly improving your ability to progress through the enticing world of Boxed Out.

Vibrant palettes and infinitely complex puzzles


Like an alluring painting, each player’s perception of the game reflects a projection of oneself onto the narrative while also breathing life into an inanimate object. So next time you are on the brink of being bored, say, at an airport daydreaming. Don’t settle: Bored? Just Boxed Out!
